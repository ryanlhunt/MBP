About

Education

I have got a degree from UC Berkeley in Urban Studies with a minor in Social and Cultural Factors in Design. I've taught classes on City Planning and Urbanism and I occasionally give lectures on my research and methods.

Location

Currently I work out of Berkeley, CA and much of my work has involved cities in California but I have a strong network of community allies spread around the country that can help inform the process of community work.

Housing

In the past, I've worked with affordable housing advocates trying to improve affordable housing stocks, I've sat on tenant landlord committees coming up with solutions to the rental housing crisis, and I've worked with neighbors and developers on making sure a project was suitable for it's proposed location.

Community Engagement

I've also done community work surrounding crime prevention and community efficacy. This meant working in partnership with law enforcement agencies, civic leaders, community members, and faith groups on creating strong community partnerships to prevent crime, violence, and intollerance.
